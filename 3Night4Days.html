3 NIGHTS 4 DAYS Package for Kashmir

3 Nights & 4 Days

Explore the beauty of KASHMIR

DAY 1 – ARRIVAL-LOCAL SIGHTSEEN-SRINAGAR NIGHT STAY. On your arrival at Srinagar Airport in evening, After your breakfast, we will drive through the heart of New Srinagar city, passing the Abdullah bridge which connects the banks of the Jehlum river, local sightseeing that includes a visit to the famous Mughal gardens of Nishat garden, Shalimar garden, Chashmashahi, Parimahal, We will explore the gardens and later we will assist you with transfers to hotel for overnight stay.

DAY 2 – SRINAGAR-SONAMARG-SRINAGAR NIGHT STAY (85 KMS) After breakfast, drive to Sonamarg. Cross the towns of Kangan, Mammar, Gagangir. Sonmarg, which means “Meadow of Gold”, is a picturesque hill station in the Kashmir Valley located in the Indian union territory of Jammu and Kashmir. Situated at an altitude of 2800 meters and adorned with a majestic glacier, meadow, dense forests, and snow-capped mountains makes Sonmarg an ideal holiday destination in Kashmir. Once you reach Sonamarg, take a 3 hour walk or horseback trip to the Thajwass glacier. After return, visit the small town market. Drive back to Srinagar in the evening and transfer to the Srinagar hotel.

DAY 3 – SRINAGAR-PAHALGHAM-SRINAGAR NIGHT STAY (98 KMS) After breakfast, we drive to Pahalgam via Pampore, Avantipura and the village of Bijbehara which remains famous as the bread basket of Kashmir. We switch from the national highway 1A at Khanabal and drive through the second largest city of Anantnag. From here the road turns scenic as we drive parallel on the Lidder river flowing from the opposite direction. After sightseeing around the mountain valley for most of the day drive back to Srinagar for overnight stay at Hotel.

DAY 4 – DEPARTURE After your breakfast, Visit the famous Shankar Acharya Temple situated on the banks of the Dal Lake and later, we will assist you with transfers to Srinagar Airport back to your home with memories.
