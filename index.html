The Heaven on Earth.
KASHMIR

Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth," is a region of unparalleled natural beauty. Nestled in the lap of the majestic Himalayas, it boasts verdant valleys, pristine lakes, and snow-capped peaks. The ever-changing seasons, from the vibrant tulips in spring to the serene snowfall in winter, add a dynamic allure to this breathtaking landscape.
