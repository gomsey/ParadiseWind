Explore the beauty of KASHMIR

Gulmarg, a jewel in the crown of Kashmir, is one of the most popular and enchanting hill stations in India. Known as the "Meadow of Flowers," Gulmarg is famed for its lush green meadows blanketed with a colorful array of wildflowers in the summer and its snow-covered slopes in the winter. Nestled in the Pir Panjal range of the Western Himalayas, it offers breathtaking views of the surrounding peaks, including Nanga Parbat, one of the highest in the world. Gulmarg is also home to one of the highest golf courses in the world and the renowned Gulmarg Gondola, the world's second-highest cable car, which takes visitors to the Apharwat Peak, offering spectacular panoramic views. A paradise for adventure enthusiasts, Gulmarg is a premier skiing destination in winter and transforms into a serene retreat for nature lovers during the warmer months.
