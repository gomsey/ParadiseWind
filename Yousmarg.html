YousMarg in Kashmir

YousMarg ❤️

Explore the beauty of KASHMIR

Yousmarg, often spelled as Yourmarg, is a captivating hill station located in the Badgam district of Kashmir, renowned for its breathtaking landscapes and tranquil ambiance. Surrounded by dense pine forests and the majestic Pir Panjal mountain range, Yousmarg is a haven for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike. The expansive meadows, often compared to the famous Swiss Alps, provide a perfect backdrop for leisurely walks, picnics, and horse riding. The Doodhganga River, with its sparkling waters, adds to the charm of this idyllic destination. Despite its relative seclusion, Yousmarg remains a hidden treasure, offering visitors an unspoiled and peaceful retreat in the heart of Kashmir.
